WealthPath Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect the information connected to our tools, free courses, and website experience.

Effective Date: November 12, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information needed to operate and improve WealthPath.

  • Personal Data You Provide: Your name or email when you contact us or submit feedback.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.
  • Third-Party Data: Non-identifiable usage data from Google Docs or Google Forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to improve your experience. This includes:

  • Responding to your questions.
  • Improving our learning tools and design.
  • Analyzing feedback to update our free course.
  • Keeping our platform secure.

3. Data Storage & Security

We use commercially accepted methods to protect your data. However, no online transmission or storage system is ever 100% secure.

4. Disclosure of Information

WealthPath does not sell, rent, or trade your information. We may share anonymized demographic data with trusted partners to help improve our content.

5. External Links (Google Docs & More)

Some of our tools rely on external services such as Google Docs and Google Forms. These sites have their own privacy policies that we do not control. Please review their policies directly.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
